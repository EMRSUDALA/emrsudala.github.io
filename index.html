About Us

EMRS Udala is committed to excellence in education, following CBSE guidelines.

Principal's Desk

Welcome to Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS), Udala. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing young minds, fostering holistic growth, and empowering tribal students with quality education.

We are committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment where students can excel academically and develop essential life skills. Through a blend of modern education and cultural values, we aim to prepare our students to face future challenges confidently.

At EMRS Udala, we believe that education is the cornerstone of progress, and we strive to instill discipline, creativity, and a sense of responsibility in our students. Together, let us continue to build a brighter future.

Warm regards,
Abhai Kumar
Principal, EMRS Udala
